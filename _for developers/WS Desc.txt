[h1]/// Any car, any map. Race, chase or escape ///[/h1]

[b]Unit Vehicles[/b] is a Sandbox-oriented addon that allows players, in both Multiplayer with others or Singleplayer with AI, to engage in high-speed pursuits as or against police and thrilling races on any map.

[b][url=https://discord.gg/kQuzmjZaPQ] >>> JOIN OUR DISCORD SERVER <<<[/url][/b]

[h3] Requirements [/h3]
Before we get started: [b]Decent Vehicle - Basic AI[/b] and [b]Glide // Styled's Vehicle Base[/b] is [b]REQUIRED[/b] for Unit Vehicles to function properly.

[hr][/hr]
[h1] Features [/h1]
[h3] Races [/h3]
[list]
	[*] Participate in Sprint or Circuit racing against your friends or AI!
	[*] Fight your opponents with various Pursuit Techs!
	[*] Create your own Races using the [b]Race Manager[/b] tool!
[/list]
[h3] Pursuits [/h3]
[list]
	[*] Participate in thrilling Pursuits as a Racer or a Unit!
	[*] Utilize Pursuit Breakers to keep the Units off your back!
	[*] Fight the Racers or Units with various Pursuit Techs!
	[*] Up to 10 Heat Levels - the higher the level, the tougher the Units!
	[*] Create your own Pursuit Breakers, Roadblocks and Units using the [b]Unit Manager[/b] tool!
[/list]

        [th]Question[/th]
        [th]Answer[/th]
    [/tr]
    [tr]
        [td]There are no races, Units or anything![/td]
        [td]You will need to create all of the main assets yourself. Alternatively, you can utilize the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3515525556]Starter Data Pack[/url] to get you started with a bunch of presets![/td]
